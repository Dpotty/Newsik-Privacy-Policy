
Privacy Policy — Newsik
Last updated: May 13, 2026

---
Overview

Newsik is a music tracking and social discovery app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

---
Data We Collect

Music & Listening Data
Your Apple Music listening history (play counts, songs, albums, artists) and statistics such as total minutes listened and top tracks. This data is sourced from your Apple Music library via the MediaPlayer and MusicKit frameworks.

Account Information
Your email address and password if you sign up with email, your name and profile photo if you sign in with Apple or Google, and the username you choose within the app.

Usage Data
Songs and artists you share with friends, friend connections you make within the app, and your profile settings such as theme, Ghost Mode, and privacy preferences.

Device Data
A push notification token to deliver friend request and song share notifications, and basic device information required by Firebase.

---
Data We Do Not Collect

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not collect your location. We do not access your contacts unless you explicitly grant permission for friend discovery. We do not access any Apple Music data beyond what you authorize via the system permission prompt.

---
How We Use Your Data

Your listening history is used to generate your personal stats, monthly recaps, and leaderboard ranking. Your account information is used to identify you and display your profile to friends. Shared songs appear in your friends' Discover feed. Friend connections power the social features including friend profiles and shared songs. Your push token is used solely to send notifications for friend requests and shared songs.

---
Data Storage

Your listening stats and social data are stored in Firebase Firestore, a cloud database operated by Google. Your account is authenticated via Firebase Authentication. Profile photos are stored securely and linked to your account. Music data synced across your devices uses CloudKit, operated by Apple.

---
Third-Party Services

Newsik uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies: Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and push notifications; Apple MusicKit for music catalog, artwork,and previews; RevenueCat for subscription management; and Last.fm for artist listener counts.

---
Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers. Your public profile — username, profile photo, and top stats — is visible to your friends within the app. If you set your profile to Private, your stats are hidden from all other users. Ghost Mode hides your currently playing song and recent listening activity from all users, including friends.

---
Data Retention

You can delete your play history at any time from Settings. You can delete your account by contacting us, which removes all your data from our servers. Cached data on your device can be cleared by
uninstalling the app.

---
Children's Privacy

Newsik is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---
Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, or withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

---
Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

For any questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at newsikappservice@gmail.com
